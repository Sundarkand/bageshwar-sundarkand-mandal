About Our Mandal

🛕 Bageshwar Bala Ji Sundarkand Mandal, Nagla Dallu

Based in the pious village of Nagla Dallu, our Mandal is dedicated to spreading the divine vibrations of Shri Hanuman Ji through the regular recitation of the Sundarkand.

We organize weekly Sundarkand Paths, special events on auspicious occasions, and community gatherings to build a strong spiritual community rooted in devotion, service, and brotherhood.

Our mission: Jai Shri Ram — Jai Bajrangbali — to bring peace, positivity, and divine blessings to every household.
